CHAR DHAM

Nicknamed ‘Land of the Gods’, Uttarakhand does justice to that moniker as it is home to some of the most revered temples and shrines in Hinduism.
Most famous of all are the Char Dham Temples in Uttarakhand that comprises of Kedarnath, Badrinath, Gangotri and Yamunotri..
